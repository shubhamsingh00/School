Approval and Awards

YMCA came into existence on 1st Dec. 2009 according to Haryana Act No. 21 of 2009 as per notification dated 16th September 2009. The University thus celebrates its Foundation Day on 16th September.
The Haryana State Govt. University is approved under 2f and 12(B) of UGC act. (Approval of University under 12(B) by UGC)
